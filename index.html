Elected Officials

Governor Terry McAuliffe

Terry McAuliffe is the 72nd Governor of Virginia. Since being sworn-into office, Governor McAuliffe has aggressively focused on building a new Virginia economy.

Senator Mark Warner

Senator Warner was elected to the U.S. Senate in November 2008 and reelected to a second term in November 2014. He serves on the Senate Finance, Banking, Budget, and Rules Committees as well as the Select Committee on Intelligence, where he is the Vice Chairman.

Democratic Committee Officers

Suzanne Bowen - Chairwoman

Suzanne Bowen moved to Pulaski in 2002 and opened her law practice in 2004. Before becoming a lawyer, Bowen had two decades experience in social services. She was a social worker in Rhode Island and moved to Colorado to oversee establishment of group homes for the mentally disabled. She earned her law degree from the University of Denver.

Mike Reis - Vice-Chairman

Mike Reis is an attorney practicing in Pulaski County and an active contributor to the Blog "The View From Peak's Knob".

Melinda Worrell - Treasurer

Melinda Worrell is also Pulaski County Treasurer.

Paige Cash - Secretary

Paige Cash is a Professor of Englisg at New River Community College and a School Board member from the Robinson district.

Timothy Goff - Technology and Web

Tim Goff relocated to Pulaski in 2011 to start a family with his Pulaski native wife. He is a software developer by day, and raising two amazing kids the rest of the time. Tim is also a contributor to the Blog "The View From Peak's Knob".
